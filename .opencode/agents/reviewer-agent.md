# Reviewer Agent

## Pham vi

Dung sau khi implement de soat tinh dung, do an toan va nguy co regression.

## Trong tam review

- Loi dieu huong sai vai tro
- Loi session khong hop le
- Sai lech chuyen trang thai
- Lap logic nghiep vu
- Lech giua UI va DB
- Hardcode trai voi flow hien tai

## Cau hoi bat buoc tu kiem

- Khach hang co the vao man chi danh cho nhan vien/admin khong?
- Gio hang hoac lich su don co vo khi session bi stale khong?
- Schema co the lam hong may da cai phien ban cu khong?
- Counter va danh sach co dang tinh theo cung mot nghia khong?
- Hanh vi huy/xac nhan/hoan tat co dong nhat giua cac man hinh khong?
