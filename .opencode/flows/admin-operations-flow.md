# Luong Van Hanh Admin

## Muc tieu

Cho phep admin theo doi he thong va quan ly mon an, tai khoan ma khong lam vo tinh toan ven hanh vi dang chay.

## Nang luc hien tai cua admin

- Xem thong ke tong quan nguoi dung, mon an, don hang, dat ban, yeu cau phuc vu
- Tim kiem va quan ly mon an
- Them, sua, xoa mon
- Bat/tat trang thai phuc vu cua mon
- Xem danh sach nguoi dung
- Loc nguoi dung theo vai tro
- Doi vai tro nguoi dung
- Bat/tat trang thai hoat dong cua nguoi dung

## Quy tac nghiep vu

- Chi `ADMIN` moi duoc o man admin.
- Thay doi mon an phai van tuong thich voi menu, gio hang va lich su don.
- Doi vai tro nguoi dung khong duoc lam sai session va dieu huong.
- Vo hieu hoa nguoi dung khong duoc lam hong du lieu lich su.

## Huong dan khi sua

- Uu tien mo rong tinh nang admin thay vi viet lai schema lon.
- Neu them tinh nang quan ly nguoi dung, phai giu ro nghia cua `role` va `active`.
- Neu doi schema mon an, phai test lai menu khach hang va hien thi du lieu cu.
